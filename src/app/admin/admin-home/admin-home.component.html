You can review some of the tips and guidelines below before publishing new content.

  • Pitch us an original idea. If it is a topic we have already covered, tell us why it needs to be revisited.
  • For photo essays, send us a brief pitch that is similar to the one for articles. The same applies for videos.
  • Wherever possible, do send us some clips of your previous work so we have an understanding of your writing style.
  • We will review your content pitch and if we like it we will get in touch with you via the email provided with next steps.
  • If we commission your content pitch our usual pay $50 for articles and $100 for visual content.
  • If you have any questions or queries you can email us on contact@examdocs.com.
